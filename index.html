About Me

Hello my name is Cody, I started programming just a few months ago and have found a great love for all things Code. I have been through a coding boot camp by the name of Boise Code Works. There I learned basic HTML 5 and CSS, I also am fluent in JavaScript and C#. I have a appreciation for programming and love to put all of my time and effort towards it.


As I have just started coding I have had no previous work experience. When I was 18 I left to serve a two year service mission for my Church. This is was based in Moscow Russia. I have spent the last 2 years of my life in Russia. There I learned how to help people in all kinds of different situations, and how to organize activities and meetings. I also am fluent in Russian and have a love for learning because of it. Before I went to Russia I have always struggled to learn. Being in a Russia and forcing to me learn Russian or not be able to communicate made me need to find a way to learn. With the help of different people who new Russian and my own desire I was able to become fluent in the language. I also gained a lot for coding in Russia. Many Russians are computer programmers and I spoke with many of them.

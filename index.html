About Me

There I learned HTML 5 and CSS, I also am fluent in JavaScript and C#. I have a appreciation for programming and love to put all of my time and effort towards it. I work hard and am very good with people.


As I have just started coding I have had no previous work experience. When I was 18 I left to serve a two year service mission for my Church. This is was based in Moscow Russia. I also am fluent in Russian and have a love for learning because of it. Before I went to Russia I have always struggled to learn. Many Russians are computer programmers and I spoke with many of them.


When I am not coding I love to spend time with my Family. We do a lot of things together like going on hikes and going out to eat. I love video games and my little brother and I can spend a good amount of time playing together. Despite this I still know how to prioritize my time so I can be the most effective with the time I have.
